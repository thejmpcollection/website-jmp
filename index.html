Concept

The JMP Collection redefines the meaning of luxury hospitality. Our concept is rooted in the conviction that excellence, design, and sustainability are not separate ambitions but inseparable parts of the same experience. Inspired by the principles of the New European Bauhaus, JMP embodies a future where sustainability is elevated through beauty, culture, and innovation.

At the heart of this vision lies the Oikία — a signature Eco Tiny House, crafted in wood and designed to harmonize with its natural surroundings. Built with renewable technologies and a focus on design, they will serve as living examples of how luxury can evolve responsibly.

Beyond accommodation, JMP positions itself as a cultural destination. In combination with art, architecture, and education, we aspire to inspire our guests and partners to become advocates for environmental awareness and sustainable living. Every stay is transformed into a meaningful journey — immersive, enriching, and unforgettable.

The JMP Collection is not only a hospitality project. It is a scalable model for the future of sustainable luxury and global demand for conscious travel. For investors, this means entering a market at the intersection of eco-innovation, cultural relevance, and long-term growth, where hospitality becomes both a business and a legacy.

Oikία

Oikía is the cornerstone of JMP’s eco-hospitality vision: sustainable wooden Tiny Houses that merge refined guest experience with long-term investment value. Each Oikía is handmade in Greece, created by our team and lead architect Efthimia Kapsali. Guests and investors may choose from our carefully developed models or work with us to design a bespoke version tailored to a unique setting or concept.

For guests, Oikía is a sanctuary of simplicity and elegance — intimate spaces that invite reconnection with nature, culture, and self. For investors, Oikía is a scalable, adaptable, and future-proof asset, designed on circular principles that reduce costs, extend lifecycle value, and align with the global rise of sustainable tourism.

More than accommodation, Oikía is both a crafted experience and a replicable framework — where hospitality becomes an enriching journey for guests and a resilient growth opportunity for partners.

Partnerships

The JMP Collection represents a scalable model of sustainable luxury hospitality. At its foundation will be Oikía — eco-designed Tiny Houses that set a new benchmark for design, comfort, and environmental responsibility.

For investors, JMP offers an opportunity to enter a growing market where demand for conscious travel is accelerating globally. The concept is designed for replication across multiple destinations, ensuring long-term growth, adaptability, and competitive distinction.

Together with our partners, we aim to build not only a profitable venture, but a lasting legacy in the future of luxury hospitality.
